About Us


Who are we?

Tales' n' Rhymes is a company that aims to expose the true meaning and origins of children's Fairy Tales and Nursery Rhymes. We not only inform viewers about the origins of Nursery Rhymes and Fairy Tales, but we also compare them with the modern version.


Donations

Our company gives away more than half of its profits to charity and the homeless. We also donate to medical research, stopping global warming and renewable energy. Please send us an email on our contact us page if you would like to give a donation.
